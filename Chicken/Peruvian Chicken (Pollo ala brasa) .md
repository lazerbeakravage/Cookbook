- 1 tbsp paprika (not smoked)

- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp white vinegar
- 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1 heaping tsp aji panca
- juice from 1 lime
- half a bottle of dark beer (Modelo negro/Guiness/Cusquena dark)
