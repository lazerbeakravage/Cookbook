## Ingredients
- 5 tbsp flour
- ½ tbsp salt
- ½ tbsp paprika
- ½ tbsp pepper
- 2 cloves garlic

- 2 cups sweet florio marsala (50/50 sweet, dry)
- 2 cups swanson cooking chicken stock

- 2 tbsp butter

## It's cooking time!
1. Cook marsala to syrup
2. Flambe? (wheeeee)
3. Add chicken stock
4. Simmer and add chicken back to sauce
