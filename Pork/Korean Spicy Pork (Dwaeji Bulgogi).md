## Ingredients
- 1 pound pork belly 
- ¼ cup spicy pepper paste (CJ Haechandle - gochujang) 
- 1 tbsp soy (4x takeout sushi packets) 
- 1 tbsp sugar 
- 2 tbsp red pepper flakes 
- Ground black pepper 
- 1 tbsp sesame oil 
- Cloves of garlic 
- ¼ onion 
- White button mushrooms
