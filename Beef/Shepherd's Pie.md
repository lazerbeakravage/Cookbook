## Ingredients
- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 carrots
- 1/2 onion
- Half bag corn 
- Half bag peas 
- 2 clove garlic 
- 1/2 bottle Guinness 
- 1.5 tbl flour 
- 1 cup chicken stock 
- 1 bay leaf 
- Some thyme 
- 2 heaping tsp tomato paste 
<br></br>
- Potatoes
- Butter
- Milk

### Beef
1. Brown beef with oil and butter
2. Add corn/peas/onion/carrots/garlic (blended?).  Cook till caramelized
3. Add tomato paste
4. Add butter and flour
5. Deglaze with beer
6. Add chicken stock/bay leaf/thyme
